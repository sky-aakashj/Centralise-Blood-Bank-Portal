About us



Blood Bank Society is an organization which aims to fight against fanatic activities.It aims to enhance the humanistic elements of each individual and prepare them to be tolerant, responsive, accountable and responsible social citizen.Today, blood banks collect blood and separate it into its various components so they can be used most effectively according to the needs of the patient.


Blood Bank Society promotes the awareness of blood donation among the public. It is committed to stay ahead of all linguistic-rational-religious-political differences and shall be fully focusing its objectives in health care activities, with technology.
